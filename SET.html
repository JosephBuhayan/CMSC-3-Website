STUDENT EVALUATION FOR TEACHING EFFECTIVENESS (SET) For FIRST Semester A.Y. 2024-2025

The Student Evaluation of Teaching (SET) will be administered this semester using the instrument that the UP Mindanao University Council and UP System had approved. The result of the SET will be for DEVELOPMENTAL PURPOSES ONLY. That is, to improve remote learning and teaching and not for decision-making on faculty promotion, awards, tenure, appointment, and renewals. It will be one of the feedback mechanisms on our delivery of remote learning this first semester.

The SET rating scale is from 1 to 5 with a provision of NA in case the item is not applicable. Your sincere and honest feedback is very much appreciated. Your responses will be anonymous and will not be linked to you as an individual.

Instructions. Please provide a SET response for all your subjects. For subjects that have two or more teachers, please evaluate each faculty separately. You may proceed by clicking or tapping the "Evaluate" corresponding to your enrolled course. Please take note that you cannot edit your responses as soon as your evaluation has been submitted. Thank you very much.
