STUDENT EVALUATION FOR TEACHING EFFECTIVENESS (SET) For FIRST Semester A.Y. 2024-2025

UP Student Course Effectiveness Evaluation (UPSCEE) (*Note to IT / OUR: You may revise this part of the instruction as needed (e.g., use a dropdown menu)). Instructions for the student: Thoughtful student feedback can help improve course effectiveness. The UP Student Course Effectiveness Evaluation (UPSCEE) is designed to yield student feedback on non-teaching aspects of course effectiveness. Your cooperation is highly appreciated in this regard. Kindly accomplish the UPSCEE for every course you are being asked to evaluate. For the course you are evaluating now, you will be presented with 18 statements describing the course. Please rate each statement on a scale of 1 to 5 (1 being 'Strongly disagree' and 5 being 'Strongly agree') to indicate your agreement. Afterwards, kindly answer the two qualitative questions on the course's strengths and areas for improvement. Anonymity Guarantee for the UPSCEE The anonymity of student responses to the UPSCEE is guaranteed. No responses can be traced back to specific respondents.
